About Me:

As a highly skilled SDET (Software Development Engineer in Test) and Test Automation Engineer, I specialize in the ERP, Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance sectors, ensuring the highest standards of quality control for software products. With a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from REVA University, I possess a robust foundation in programming languages and testing methodologies. My expertise encompasses Automation Testing, API Testing, Selenium, ETL Testing, and Functional Testing, which I utilize to maintain the reliability and precision of critical systems. I design and implement automation suites using advanced tools such as Cypress, Playwright, and Cucumber, and employ efficient testing strategies like Behavior-Driven Development (BDD). My contributions to various projects have resulted in secure and compliant software solutions within the highly regulated BFSI sector, consistently meeting customer expectations and quality benchmarks. I am dedicated to excellence, meticulous in my work, and committed to continuous learning and skill enhancement.
